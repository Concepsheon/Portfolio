Nightlife App

This is a nightlife coordination app that I built while attending bootcamp.

Book Club

A book club web app where logged in users can start and join book clubs. Users can search for a book club to start or join an existing one and share their thoughts and ideas about the book with other members.

Made with MongoDB, Express, AngularJS, and NodeJS

Post App

This is a posting web app similar to reddit or hackernews. You can view all posts and omments and as a logged in user you can post your own posts and links. Logged in users can also comment and upvote posts and comments that they like.

This web app was made with MongoDB, Express, AngularJS, and NodeJS
